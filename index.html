Instructions
  • Hunt ducks as they fly around the marsh
  • You have three shots per duck. If all shots are unsuccessful the target is marked as a miss
  • Each round has 10 ducks each. You may advance to the next round if you shoot the required number of ducks
  • The ducks fly faster and the number of required targets increases in later rounds
  • Aim for the leaderboards
